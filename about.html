Our Story

From humble beginnings to Singapore’s leading boutique wine distributor.

Who We Are

Founded in 2015, Harbor Cellars began as a small team of wine enthusiasts with one mission — to connect Singapore’s wine lovers with the world’s finest vineyards. Over the years, our dedication to quality, authenticity, and personalized service has built strong relationships with both clients and winemakers alike.

Today, we proudly represent over 50 international labels, curating wines that express character, craftsmanship, and terroir. We believe wine is more than a beverage — it’s a story of heritage, soil, and human artistry.

Our Mission

To enrich Singapore’s wine culture by offering handpicked, sustainably produced wines and building meaningful connections between vineyards and consumers.

Our Vision

To become Asia’s most trusted name in boutique wine distribution, known for integrity, education, and excellence in every pour.
